      01 / Services
      
What I take on

      
Most engagements start with a build or a migration and grow into the measurement work that keeps a site honest. Pick one piece or hand me the whole stack.

    

    

      

        build
        
Web design & development

        
Marketing sites and landing pages designed and built to spec — clean, fast, and ready for the people who have to update them after launch.

      

      

        platform
        
Webflow & WordPress builds

        
Production sites on the platform that fits your team — visual CMS in Webflow, or WordPress and Elementor when you need it.

      

      

        migrate
        
Site migrations & rebuilds

        
Platform moves and full rebuilds with redirects, content, and tracking carried over intact — handed back to you with ownership clean.

      

      

        measure
        
Analytics & tracking

        
GA4 and Google Tag Manager set up properly: events that map to real goals, cross-domain tracking, and filtering that keeps the data trustworthy.

      

      

        rank
        
SEO, AEO & technical audits

        
Indexing, structure, and Core Web Vitals audits with a prioritized fix list — plus Answer Engine Optimization so your content surfaces in AI-generated answers and featured snippets, not just traditional search results.

      

      

        consent
        
Cookie & consent compliance

        
Consent-mode setups that actually gate your tags — CookieYes, Cookiebot, and GTM wired so nothing fires before opt-in.

      

      

        martech
        
HubSpot & martech setup

        
Forms, contact properties, and the plumbing between your site and the tools your marketing team lives in.

      

      

        email
        
Email marketing automation

        
Lifecycle and nurture flows connected to your forms and CRM, with the tracking to see what converts.

      

      

        retainer
        
Ongoing maintenance

        
A standing arrangement for updates, fixes, and the small things that pile up — so your site doesn't quietly rot between projects.

      03 / About
      
One person, the whole pipeline

    

    

      

        
Mott Design is a freelance studio working with medical device, genomics, and healthcare-technology companies — the kind of clients where a marketing site has to be fast, compliant, and measurable, not just pretty.

        
Because it's one person from kickoff to launch, the build and the tracking are never someone else's problem. The site gets designed, developed, migrated, instrumented, and handed back to you with the analytics and consent already working — and documentation so your team isn't stuck.

        
The work spans the full marketing-technology stack: design and development, GA4 and Tag Manager, SEO, consent compliance, HubSpot, and email automation. One point of contact, no handoffs between agencies.
